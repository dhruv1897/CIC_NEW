# PULSE Growth Team — Example Round-Table (demo on sample data)

🎯 **QUESTION:** What should you focus on this week to grow PULSE?

## 📊 ANALYST — the numbers
- **1,009 visitors across 13 logged days**, climbing steadily (42 → 126/day) — roughly **3× in two weeks**. Real upward trend.
- **Signup conversion 4.8%** (48 signups ÷ 1,009 visitors) — strong for a news product, but the base is still small (48 people total).
- The **one day you didn't publish, traffic fell to 0**. Publishing days clearly drive the traffic.
- **Revenue ≈ $146 total, ~$0.15 per visitor**, almost all from a single $25/week sponsor plus tiny subscriptions — thin and concentrated.
- **Metric to move this week: total signups** (your owned audience). Everything else compounds off it.

## 📣 MARKETER — growth take
- **The site isn't live yet (the URL 404s).** That's the #1 blocker — nothing grows until readers can reach it. Finish GitHub Pages.
- Add a dead-simple **email signup + "forward to a friend"** prompt. Morning Brew's referral loop drove ~30% of new subscribers and started from exactly this organic forwarding behavior.
- Pick **one** daily distribution channel (e.g. one social post per briefing at a fixed time) to reinforce the daily habit.

## 💰 SALES — money take
- At ~100 visitors/day it's **too early for heavy ads** — protect the experience. Keep the one sponsor.
- The **email list is the revenue engine**; every signup now is future monetizable reach. Prioritize capture over squeezing revenue today.
- Add **1–2 relevant affiliate links** in the briefing — the lowest-effort incremental revenue at this size.

## ⚖️ TENSIONS
- Sales could add ad units for quick cash, but the Marketer warns it dents a fragile early experience — **hold off**.
- The Marketer wants new channels; the Analyst notes **consistency matters more right now** (the gap day proved it).

## ✅ MANAGER — final plan
1. **Get the site live this week** (finish GitHub Pages → `/docs`). → *watch: the URL actually loads.* Nothing else matters until this works.
2. **Add an email signup + "forward to a friend" prompt** to the page. → *watch: signups per week.*
3. **Never miss a publish day** — the gap day cost you traffic; consistency is the product. → *watch: your work streak.*

**Ignore for now:** more ad slots, paid subscriptions, multi-channel marketing. Too early — they'd cost focus and hurt the experience.
